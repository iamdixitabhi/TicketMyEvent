Delhi Daredevils

Delhi Daredevils The Delhi Daredevils are a franchise cricket team representing the city of Delhi in the Indian Premier League (IPL). Founded in 2008 as one of the eight founding teams of the IPL, the Delhi franchise is owned by the GMR Group. Their home ground is the Feroz Shah Kotla Ground in Delhi .The team was table toppers twice (an IPL record shared only with the Chennai Super Kings) and qualified for Champions League T20 twice, making the semifinals in the 2012 edition and is looking forward to a spectacular season in the 10th edition of the IPL.

RAMAN RAGHAV REVIEW BY CRITICS


"The Times Of India" : Raman (Nawazuddin Siddiqui) reveres Raman Raghav, a pyschopath killer who terrorised the Mumbai in the 1960s. He is looking for Raghav; his other half. Raman finds his 'soulmate' in ACP Raghav, the cop investigating the murders committed by him.


"IMDB" :Set in present day Mumbai the story follows the life of a serial killer Ramanna who is inspired by an infamous serial killer from the 1960s, Raman Raghav. His strange obsession with Raghavan, a young cop keeps growing as he closely follows him without his knowledge and often creates situations where both of them come face to face.


"Hindustan Times" :Raman Raghav 2.0 is not about how life treats human beings, it is about a Satan reincarnate who walks among humans. No Bollywood-style revenge or anger, just pure sadism. Ramanna’s purity, despite his cruelty is spine-chilling, especially when juxtaposed with Raghavan’s uncontrolled violence.
